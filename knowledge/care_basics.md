# Core pet care foundations

Daily plans should balance food, hydration, movement, rest, and enrichment.

- Dogs usually benefit from structured activity windows and predictable feeding times.
- Cats generally prefer routine and shorter enrichment sessions across the day.
- Senior pets often need shorter sessions and additional recovery breaks.
- Young pets tolerate higher activity but still need structured rest and hydration windows.

When scheduling, prioritize medication and feeding windows first, then place exercise and play.
