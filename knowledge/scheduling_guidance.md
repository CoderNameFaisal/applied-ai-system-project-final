# Scheduling and conflict guidance

When timed tasks overlap:

1. Keep medication at fixed times unless a veterinarian has advised flexibility.
2. Prefer moving enrichment tasks before moving feeding windows.
3. Preserve a buffer between intense exercise and feeding.
4. For anxious pets, avoid stacking multiple demanding tasks back-to-back.

If owner time is limited, high-priority safety tasks should remain in the plan over optional enrichment.
