# Breed, species, age, and habit adjustments

Scheduling should incorporate species, breed tendencies, age, and known habits.

- Breed tendencies can influence activity intensity and pacing.
- Species and age should drive default duration and timing assumptions.
- Habits like "hides at noon" or "high energy at dawn" should influence when tasks are placed.

When user notes conflict with pet habits, suggest schedule changes that respect those habits while protecting high-priority care tasks.
